Overview |

I am ZhuoFan,17 years old this year. My full name is Chen ZhuoFan,陈卓凡. Since young, I have a deep interest in software-related stuff as I am quite attracted by the UI and the functionality of programs. I always wonder how these things ever work. Hence, I have started to go into android development when I was in Primary 6, throughout the years, I have learned a lot of skills and knowledge that allow me to embark on various android development projects like LineageOS and Remix Resurrection OS. In 2018, with groups of developers that have the same interest, we established a project called exthmUI which is a custom ROM based on LineageOS but with additional features by us. Through these projects, I have learned quite a lot of things. Aside from the new knowledge, I also learned to persevere while I met errors. In 2019, I have a friend that encouraged me to learn C languages. Hence, I have started learning C programming since then. I have read various books and watch videos about it and have tried to put what I learn into practice. Every time I manage to do something that I am not able to do in the past, I will feel kind of happy as I feel that I have progressed. This encourages me to keep digging into the C language. Now I can make some small programs using it.In 2021,during the long holiday after O'Level ,I have started my learning on JavaScript and HTML.
