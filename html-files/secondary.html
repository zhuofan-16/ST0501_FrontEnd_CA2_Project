Some Words After
|

I was a student of Dunearn Secondary School from 2017 to 2020. I have enjoyed myself in these 4 years of secondary school life. In these 4 years, I have experienced a lot of things, some are bad, some are good, but all of these have allowed me to mature into the person I am today --one perceptive to the needs of others and one who dares to lead with passion and purpose. I am thankful to all my teachers and friends that have walked with me on this 4-year journey. 4 years is really fast, till today I could still remember the summer that we all studied hard in classes, trying to understand everything the teacher said and to put it into practice. I could also remember the nights when everyone was sleeping and we are trying to complete the practice papers that have never seemed to have an end….

Learner Today, Leader Tomorrow

In these 4 years, I think I have grown especially in terms of leadership. My school has transformed me into a leader, till today I am still really thankful to the teachers that have trusted me and given me the opportunity to lead. In 2017, as a Secondary 1 student, I was lucky to get selected to become a junior student councillor which enabled me to learn a lot of things like to take initiative, be more responsible, and speak confidently. I have taken all the morning duties and the events I am participating in seriously. I always get to learn more things as the debrief after every event has allowed me to reflect on things that I have not done so well which enable me to correct myself and be a better leader. In 2019, I was nominated and chosen to be part of the Executive Committee of the 15th Student Council. This means that I will have more responsibilities in hand. As an exco member, I have always tried my best to plan the events as detailed as possible and to execute them smoothly. Through the events I have planned and led, I have a further understanding of leadership skills.I knew more about the responsibility in me.Additionally,I also learn more about proposal making and how to be flexible when carrying out events. I learn to react fast to unexpected situations, I learn to take care of my teammates, I learn to listen to other people’s opinions. While planning and executing events, I also forged strong bonds with my fellow councillors.As a senior , I always tried my best to teach my junior everything I have learned just like what my seniors have taught me. Aside from my student council role, I am also a physics representative for 3 years, a geographic representative for 1 year, and a staff sergeant in NPCC.I am really thankful to the everyone that has trusted me.

Lifelong Friends

I am glad that through the 4 years I have met a group of friends that can be trusted. Whenever we have projects or activities that require multiple people, they will be the first ones to join me. During recess or after school, we will always hang out together in the library or the school canteen. I could still remember that it was these people that had to stay up until 2 am with me to finish work or projects together. Whenever I have any questions or needs any help, I am confident to say that they are there.We have even formed our group called the “Dunearn Executive Committee”, the name may sound weird but as you can see from the name, we hope that we can progress together as a whole and make our school proud. Well, although at the end of 4 years, we didn't manage to achieve something great but at least we tried. We have gone to a lot of competitions together (although most of them we get participation)but we enjoy working hard together for a common goal. To enrich our life in secondary school, we also go to the science center to be a volunteer regularly, till today, we have volunteered more like 150 hours in the science center.

Lastly,Conclusion

In this school,I have gone through a lot of fun and meaningful events/projects like Geographic Investigation,Self Initial VIA Project,CNY Celebrations,SOOP program，Science and technology challenges and more.I have also had a lot of my first times, like my first time to dance, to speak in front of the whole school, lead on projects, going to a camp, etc. These are the things that I have never imagined in the past, I am thankful that Dunearn has allowed me to experience so many things and made me grown not only in terms of knowledge but also in character. If I were to choose my secondary school again, I am very sure that I will choose Dunearn again :)
